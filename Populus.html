Populus

Populus was a startup by fellow mates at my university. The idea behind the app is to have a sports fantasy app but it is centralized for all sports into a single application.

My role as a part of the team was to optimize and icnrease the functionality of the application. During my time, I was able to successfully redesigned the Full Stack Architecture, resulting in a 30% improvement in app performance and scalability. I collaborated closely with front-end engineers to meet and enhance necessary design requirements, ensuring a seamless user experience. Additionally, I expanded networking opportunities, which contributed to a 15% boost in user connectivity within the app. By removing redundant API calls in the framework, I achieved faster backend responses and implementations, further enhancing the overall efficiency of the application.
